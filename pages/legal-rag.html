AI Legal Document Assistant

A Retrieval-Augmented Generation system that helps users upload legal documents, ask questions, retrieve relevant clauses, and identify risks from lengthy contracts.

View GitHub Contact Me

Problem

Legal documents are lengthy and difficult to review manually. Users need a faster way to search clauses, understand risks, and get contextual answers from contracts.

Solution

Built a RAG-based assistant that extracts document text, creates embeddings, stores them in FAISS, retrieves relevant clauses, and generates grounded answers.

Key Features

  • PDF document parsing
  • Semantic search using FAISS
  • Clause-based question answering
  • Risk detection from legal documents
  • Source-grounded responses

Tech Stack

Python • Streamlit • FAISS • Sentence Transformers • Hugging Face • RAG • PDFPlumber
